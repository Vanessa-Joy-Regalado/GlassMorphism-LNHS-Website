School Name : Landy National High School

Address: Landy, Santa Cruz, Marinduque

Curricular Class : Junior High School with Senior High School

Date of Operation : Sunday, January 01, 1967

Landy National High School Formerly – is a DepED Managed Monograde Public Secondary School Located in 2nd District of MARINDUQUE. School with no Annexes.

Landy National High School is an autonomous public secondary school in the Province of Marinduque offering complete Science and Regular Class Curriculum for Junior High School and three strand for Senior High School like STEM, HUMSS, TVL -HE and ICT.

Given the high academic performance of Landy National High School students, the school aims to inspire them to take pride in their sense of identity as Filipinos so they realize the value of giving back and paying forward to our country and our people. The faculty, administration and staff, family, and other stakeholders nurture and provide a sense of security to our learners that enables them to become well-rounded individuals who have a deep sense of commitment and empathy.
