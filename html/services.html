LNHS

Landy National High School (LNHS) is a public high school, founded in February 1968 located in Santa Cruz, Marinduque recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd). They promoting a cultural-based, gender sensitive , safe and motivating environment for the students, teachers, administrator and staff. It implements a K-12 basic education program that offers Academic Strands such as Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) and Accounting, as well as Technical-Vocational-Livelihood tracks.
